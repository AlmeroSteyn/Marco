Wood rot specialisation

We are specialised in the repair of wood rot and we work with high quality products to treat your window frames.

The affected area is removed and repaired, which leaves the structural integrity of the window frame intact. When repair is not possible, we can supply and install new window frames as well.

Maintenance and home improvement

We can completely rebuild the interior and exterior of your home according to your wishes. This means new doors, windows, window frames, radiators, closets or even a new veranda, wooden floors etc. We always use high quality products.

We can also assist in the maintenance of your house. Regular maintenance means less large repairs.

Simply put: when it comes to wood, we are your best choice.

Renovation / Restoration

We renovate old buildings to conform to modern standards using both modern and antique materials and techniques.

We can retain the antique look and feel of your house or we can give it a brand new modern look. Also think about glass and roof insulation.

Thereby we restore monumental houses, ensuring that they retain their characteristic such as antique window frames with authentic profiles.

Who are we?

Carpentry Marco Burger is a small business in the centre of the Netherlands, between Woerden, Nieuwegein and Gouda.

Our company model allows us to offer you the most flexible and affordable options, with a personal touch.

Quality is of utmost importance to us.

No project is too large for us and for specialised tasks, such as paintwork, plastering, plumbing and all electrical matters, we subcontract to experts in the field with whom we have years of professional experience.
